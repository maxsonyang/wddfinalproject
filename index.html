Computer Science is Beautiful.

When you hear about computer science, you might think of 1's and 0's, or some man hunched over a computer, typing confusing green text on a command line. But computer science and its impacts on our lives and society go far beyond that, from the applications we depend on each day, helping us ask deep and compelling questions about our world, or helping us create better, smarter solutions.
In CS10, you'll get a taste of all sorts of things that make computing beautiful, from building your own projects from scratch, to exploring the some exciting applications of computing so that you too, may find something beautiful in the world of computing.

Discover CS with a Diverse Community.

One awesome thing about CS10 is that we're one of the most diverse CS courses on campus, from majors, year, background, to goals for learning CS. We pride ourselves in serving traditionally under-represented minorities, as well as boasting female enrollment over 50% regularly. Whether you're looking to pursue CS in the future, see if it's right for you, or are just curious, there's always a place for you in CS10.

Humans of CS10
Build Your Foundation.

Over 90% of high schools in the United States don't offer a CS course, or AP CS Principles course, and chances are you might not have been exposed to any computing principles. In CS10, we help lay down the fundamentals you need to go deeper in the field in a slower, friendlier manner. We'll be teaching core programming principles in the block-based language Snap! and transition over to Python towards the last few weeks of the class. If you're looking to prepare for courses like CS61A, CS10 is definitely an excellent way to go.
